FABLAB SAFETY

Fablab @ Singapore Polytechnic has many tools, machinery and equipment to produce your idea. The operations of some of these instruments and equipment will be taught in the course of the module, but before that, the teachers should ensure our safety in the laboratory.

All Fablab@SP users are expected to perform the safety assessment. The course records and notes are available on the SP e-learning platform in any courses. Before taking the safety assessment, we are urged to study the content.

GENERAL SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS

  • Students must abide by SP Safety Policy, Regulations and Procedures.
    SP Students' WSH Handbook 2015
  • Students using Fab Lab or any workshops should also abide by:
    Fab Lab & Workshop Safety Rules

 

Workshop Safety

Danger communications are evident in workplaces or Fab Lab to warn students of occupational risks and the safety measures to be taken in various work environments, especially while working with dangerous machinery.
